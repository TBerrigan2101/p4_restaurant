Welcome to Umai!

The home of authentic Japanese Sushi in Dublin

Sushi is simple. That's why it's so complicated.

It takes years to master and moments to enjoy. At Umai we promise that our experience and commitment to quality will not be matched

For the last 10 years we have served thousands of customers and they always come back for more. The best ingredients, a warm, cozy setting and friendly faces ensure we deliver the best sushi experience.

Our Current Menu

At Umai we pride ourselves on using the best Japanese and Irish ingredients to create sushi like no other. Our set menu of six dishes will provide you with the full sushi experience from the simplest origins to modern takes made right in front of you as you dine with us.

Sashimi

Raw seafood delicately cut into thin slices. Seafood at its purest.

Nigri

Hand-pressed bed of rice topped with a single piece of glazed seafood

Maki

Sushi rice with a variety of fillings at the center wrapped in a sheet of nori.

Uramaki

Maki but in reverse. Sushi rice on the outside, followed by a sheet of nori and sushi filling at the center.

Temaki

A combination of veggies, seafood and sauces server in a cone-shaped nori sheet.

Gunkan

Sushi wrapped around a small bed of rice and a variety of toppings.
